The Next Chapter

A A Wedding B

by

Tim Camara  &  Sarah Baugher

Once upon a time, a boy and a girl met on the first day of high school.
For her, it was love at first sight. For him, it took a bit longer than that. It wasn't really your typical storybook romance.
After graduation, some distance showed them what they had taken for granted. Although separated by 1200 miles, their relationship blossomed. So did their cell phone bills.
After 3 years, they were finally reunited for good. They moved in together, bought some Ikea furniture, and started going on adventures. They even adopted a kitty with the heart of a lion.
Now, they're inviting you to help them write the next chapter.
