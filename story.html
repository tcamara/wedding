Our Story

Chapter 1 - It's just biology

Our story begins the same way that every classical romance does: in biology class freshman year of high school. By luck of having last names that are close when arranged alphabetically, our eventual happy couple first laid eyes on each other across the scarred and graffitied lab benches of Mr. Sanford's class. Awkward introductions and many boring lectures later, our heroes become fast friends. Group projects, including one particularly clairvoyant one tracing the genetic traits our hypothetical children would inherit, served to cement their friendship.

Chapter 2 - The First "Date"

Far from being love at first sight, Tim seemed remarkably unresponsive to Sarah's initial romantic overtures. Not being one to give up just because of a clueless guy, Sarah asked Tim to accompany her to the winter ball. In a very typical response, he said "let me think about it". Not exactly a love story for the ages. The dance came and went, and involved very little actual dancing, thanks mostly in part to a very awkward Tim.

Chapter 3 - Prom

Imagine, if you will, a montage of typical high school events. Our protagonists both spent many hours running on the cross country team, building camaraderie and blisters. Sarah participated on the swim team while Tim joined all the bands. As high school drew to a close, prom, that seminal high school event, approached. Significantly older and wiser than the last time they went to a dance together, Tim and Sarah went to prom together, and only got lost in San Francisco a little bit on the way there.
