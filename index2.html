Once upon a time...

A boy and a girl met

in biology class, freshman year of high school

It wasn't really your typical storybook romance

After graduation, some distance taught them what they had taken for granted

Although separated by 1200 miles, their relationship blossomed

So did their cell phone bills

After 3 years, they were finally reunited for good

They moved in together, bought some Ikea furniture, and started going on adventures

They even adopted a kitty with velociraptor DNA

Now, they're inviting you to help them write the next chapter
